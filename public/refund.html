Refund Policy

Last updated June 21, 2026

30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy with PickAStudent Premium, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase — no questions asked.

What's covered

This policy applies to one-time purchases of PickAStudent Premium. You may request a full refund for any reason within 30 days of the date of purchase.

How to request a refund

You can request a refund in either of the following ways:

  • Reply to the receipt email you received from our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, and request a refund; or
  • Email us directly at gentlebloompress@gmail.com with the email address used at checkout.

How refunds are processed

Payments and refunds for PickAStudent are handled by Lemon Squeezy, our Merchant of Record. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take a few business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

After a refund

Once a refund is issued, your premium license will be deactivated and your account will return to the free tier. Your locally saved classes and settings remain on your device and are not affected.

Questions

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at gentlebloompress@gmail.com.
